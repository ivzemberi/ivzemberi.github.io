Profile

Hi, I'm Ivana. A UX Designer from Croatia recently moved to Osterholz-Scharmbeck, Germany. As a result, I'm still learning German at the moment.

In most of my projects, I followed the Material Design Guidelines. Recently I started exploring the Human Interface Design guidelines as well. I'm preparing myself to design a great experience for Apple platforms too.

Besides designing and drawing illustrations, I also enjoy reading and writing. While I was a student, I volunteered as a journalist in the student's newsroom. That's also where I strengthened my communication skills.

I still write when I find the time. One of my blog posts even got published in Bootcamp.
